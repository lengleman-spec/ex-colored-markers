Tertiary Colors are made by combining primary colors with their nearby secondary colors.

Complementary Colors

Complementary Colors are two colors that are opposite of each other on the color wheel.

When two complementary colors are combined, they create a shade of gray.

However, when they are placed side by side, they produce a strong visual contrast for the human eye.

It's best practice to choose one color as the dominant color. Then use it's complementary color to add accent to the page.
